${\displaystyle M ={\begin{pmatrix}-2&3\\3&-6\end{pmatrix}}}$

$$ {\displaystyle A + B = {\begin{pmatrix}1\\2\end{pmatrix}} + {\begin{pmatrix}1\\0\end{pmatrix}} = {\begin{pmatrix}1+1\\2+0\end{pmatrix}} = {\begin{pmatrix}2\\2\end{pmatrix}}} $$
